About us

Trackflaw is a startup specialising in IT security training and more specifically in offensive security.

Through strict methodologies and rigorous work requirements, Trackflaw is committed to providing concise and professional training that can be understood by both highly managerial and highly technical audiences.

Always with a view to optimal quality, no mission is subcontracted in order to guarantee the know-how and the demanding brand image of the company.

Our values

Many companies are active in the world of IT security and more particularly in the training market. It's legitimate to ask why trust Trackflaw?



A meticulous methodology

To guarantee the high quality of its training courses, Trackflaw meticulously follows an internal methodology. Each course is balanced between theoretical and practical content. Presentation material, worksheets and practical exercises are provided to stimulate and optimise the students' learning. Correction and follow-up time is included in the service.



No subcontracting

Many unscrupulous companies subcontract their training assignments for various reasons. This lack of professionalism leads to problems of follow-up and transparency for the client.

At Trackflaw, in order to guarantee the firm's know-how, high standards and brand image, as well as to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of the elements handled during audits, no mission is subcontracted. Only the firm's employees are authorised to take part in missions.
