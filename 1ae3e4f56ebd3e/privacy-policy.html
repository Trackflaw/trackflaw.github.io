Cyber Security News App - Privacy Policy

Introduction

This TikTok API Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes how TikTok collects, uses, and shares personal information about you when you use the TikTok API. By using the TikTok API, you agree to be bound by this Policy.

Definitions

TikTok" means TikTok Technology Limited, a company incorporated in the British Virgin Islands with company number 1374481. "TikTok API" means the tools and resources provided by TikTok that allow you to integrate TikTok content into your own applications and websites. "You" means the individual or entity that is using the TikTok API.

Personal information

TikTok collects the following personal information about you when you use the TikTok API: Your name and contact information, such as your email address and phone number. Your IP address and location information. Information about your use of the TikTok API, such as the types of TikTok content you access and the frequency with which you access it

How We Use Your Personal Information

TikTok uses your personal information for the following purposes: To provide you with the TikTok API. To improve the TikTok API. To send you marketing communications about TikTok products and services.

How We Share Your Personal Information

TikTok may share your personal information with the following third parties: Our service providers, who help us operate the TikTok API. Other companies that we partner with to provide you with products or services. Government agencies, if required by law.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information: You have the right to access your personal information. You have the right to correct your personal information if it is inaccurate. You have the right to delete your personal information. You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information. To exercise your rights, please contact TikTok

Data Security

TikTok takes steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These steps include: Using physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect our systems. Requiring our employees to comply with our privacy policies and procedures.

Data Retention

TikTok will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with the TikTok API and for other legitimate business purposes.
